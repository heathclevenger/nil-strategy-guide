Model notes

This is an educational planning model, not tax, legal, accounting, or investment advice. It uses simplified 2026 federal assumptions and lets you adjust key facts that should be confirmed by a qualified advisor.

  • IRS self-employment tax guidance: 15.3% SE tax, Social Security plus Medicare, with an above-the-line deduction for the employer-equivalent portion.
  • IRS S-corp compensation guidance: shareholder-employees must receive reasonable compensation before non-wage distributions.
  • 2026 assumptions in this model: $184,500 Social Security wage base, $16,100 single standard deduction, $24,500 Solo 401(k) elective deferral limit, $72,000 SEP/Solo 401(k) annual additions cap, and $360,000 retirement compensation cap.
